# Password Guide - IndabaX Kenya

---

## I Forgot My Password

1. Go to the **Login page**
2. Click **"Forgot password?"**
3. Enter your **email address**
4. Click **"Send Reset Link"**
5. Check your **email inbox** (also check spam)
6. Click the **link in the email** (expires in 1 hour)
7. Enter your **new password**
8. Click **"Reset Password"**
9. Done! Go back to login

---

## I Was Invited as Admin

1. Check your **email inbox** for invitation
2. You will see:
   - Your email address
   - A temporary password
3. Click **"Log In to Admin Panel"**
4. Enter your **email** and **temporary password**
5. You will be taken to **change your password**
6. Enter:
   - The temporary password (current)
   - Your new password
   - Confirm your new password
7. Click **"Update Password"**
8. Done! You can now use the admin panel

---

## I Want to Change My Password

1. Log in to the **Admin Panel**
2. Click **"My Profile"** in the sidebar
3. Click **"Change Password"**
4. Enter:
   - Your current password
   - Your new password
   - Confirm your new password
5. Click **"Update Password"**
6. Done!

---

## Password Rules

Your password must have:

- At least **8 characters**
- At least **1 UPPERCASE letter** (A-Z)
- At least **1 lowercase letter** (a-z)
- At least **1 number** (0-9)
- At least **1 special character** (!@#$%&*)

**Good example:** `MyPassword123!`

**Bad examples:**
- `password` (no uppercase, no number, no special)
- `PASSWORD123` (no lowercase, no special)
- `Pass1!` (too short)

## Common Problems

| Problem | What to Do |
|---------|------------|
| No email received | Check spam folder. Wait 5 minutes. Try again. |
| Link not working | Link expires after 1 hour. Request a new one. |
| "Invalid link" message | Link was already used. Request a new one. |
| Password not accepted | Make sure it follows all the rules above. |
| Can't access admin after login | You must change your password first. |
